Summer is over and hopefully it has been a rejuvenating break. The new semester at Carnegie Mellon awaits you with fresh challenges and opportunities for growth.

If your goals for the upcoming year include resumé building and experience in the working world, consider a student job at the CMU Telefund. It offers work in an on-campus location earning up to $9.75 per hour in pay, as well as the opportunity to support your university. In addition to this, you will be able to obtain new skills that are highly sought after in professional settings.

CMU Telefund is seeking enthusiastic candidates to immediately become CMU Ambassadors, where you can call alumni, parents and friends for gifts to help make our university the best place for higher education!

Please apply today by visiting cmu.thecallingcenter.com. For any questions, please contact Vince Cavataio at 412-268-4371.
